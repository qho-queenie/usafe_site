How Does it Work?


Login or Register

Add or Choose Task

You can add a new task with the "+" button, or remove a task with the remove button. Then choose one of the tasks from the picker.
Click 'Emergency Contact' at the bottom bar.

Emergency Contact

You can add a new emergency contact with the "+" button, or remove a contact with the remove button.
When you choose a specific contact for this task, click 'Send Availability Request' at the botton to check if your contact is available at the moment.
Click 'Set Timer' at the bottom bar.

Set Timer

Set the number of minutes for this task, and any additional message for your contact.
Click 'Start Task' at the bottom bar.

Start Task

Available contacts will be listed.
Once you have selected a contact, the "Start" button will appear.
Press the start button to start your task. Your contact will be notified.
If you don't check in with the "End Task" button within the time limit, your contact will be notified!
